    9Malachite Builders, HERA9 VIP, Gamebox Scam Victims

    by Robinhood - Apr 18 5239 Views 0 Comment
    Facebook

    I, like many others, found myself drawn to an investment opportunity that promised exceptional returns. HERA9, owned by CASSANDRA SHARIJANE DIMAYUGAN and managed by Rommel Basilio, claimed to offer monthly interest/commissions of 5% to 15%. With an office based in Pasay City and a junket in Pampanga designed to enhance its legitimacy, the allure of high returns was hard to resist. Unfortunately, this turned out to be a carefully crafted facade.

    At first glance, HERA9 seemed credible. They provided post-dated checks from various banks, which gave the impression of a reliable investment vehicle. However, as time went on, many of these checks bounced, revealing the true nature of the operation.

    Additionally, the organization issued notarized loan agreements signed by persona named Rommel Basilio, further convincing investors of their legitimacy. It wasnt until we began to encounter issues with these agreements and payments that we realized the extent of the deception.

    The reality hit hard when the promised returns never materialized. As we began to connect with other investors, it became clear that we were not alone in this struggle. The sense of betrayal and frustration was palpable, as many of us had placed our trust in what we thought was a legitimate investment opportunity.

    As an investor who has faced this harsh reality, I urge anyone who has fallen victim to HERA9 to join our community. Together, we can navigate the path to reclaim our investments and ensure that those responsible for this scam are held accountable.
